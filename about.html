Nice To Meet You

My name is Blake West, I started Stellr Sites for the simple purpose of helping smaller businesses afford premium websites. I've been building websites for a little over 5 years and through those years, I've seen first-hand that not every business has the same needs or budget for a website. A small restaurant or hair salon does not have the same budget as a multi-million dollar company, but that small business needs a website just as much as that million dollar company.

Working in the web design industry has taught me a lot and I've learned what works and what doesn't. What works is building a site custom to the business's needs. What doesn't work is using the same template for every site or using page builders like Wix, Squarespace, or Weebly. One of the most important factors that affects your site's traffic is loading times. If a website takes longer that 3 seconds to load, 40% of users will exit that site. Having a site custom built for your business will keep it lightweight and only use necessary resources.
